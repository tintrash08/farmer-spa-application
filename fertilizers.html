Fertilizers used for paddy

1. Urea : It is best for paddy fields.

2. Farm yard manure (FYM): It is known to maintain soil productivity longer than inorganic fertilizers.



Fertilizers used for sugarcane

1.Nitrogen : The recommended amount of nitrogen is based on 1 Kg N per ton of expected plant cane.

2.Phosphate : All applied at planting according to soil analysis.



Fertilizers used for wheat

1. Nitrogen : Rapid transformation into leachable forms

Sulphur : Wheat crop suffers from sulphur deficiency when sown in sandy soils
